Phishing Training Course

Objective: Educate participants on recognizing and responding to phishing attempts effectively.

Target Audience: Employees at all levels who need to enhance their cybersecurity awareness.

Key Topics:

  1. Understanding Phishing: Definition, types, and examples.
  2. Recognizing Phishing Attempts: Key indicators and warning signs.
  3. Responding to Phishing: Steps to take if a phishing attempt is suspected.
  4. Preventative Measures: Best practices to avoid falling victim to phishing.

Unique Features:

  • Interactive guided tours and exercises to simulate real-life phishing scenarios.
  • Immediate feedback on responses to help reinforce learning.
  • Practical tips and tools for identifying and mitigating phishing threats.

Expected Outcomes:

Participants will be able to identify phishing attacks, respond appropriately, and implement best practices.
